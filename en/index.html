🌍🕊️ ECP’AI Project: Call for Contributions

Together, let’s build innovative pedagogy and AI that serve peace, education, and humanity. 🤝💡

Welcome to the ECP’AI call for contributions 💫

This project stems from a simple conviction: artificial intelligence can be a force for good when rooted in the values of Education for a Culture of Peace 🌿.

Together, let’s combine technological innovation and educational wisdom to imagine tools, practices, and learning models with human, ethical, and sustainable impact 🌈.

🧭 Project Vision

Our ambition is to co-create a collective, open project that mobilizes actors in education, technology, and civil society around a shared objective: deploying ECP through AI, while preserving the core values of living together, kindness, and solidarity 🌱.

💡 The 4 Collaborative Workshops

🏗️ Workshop 1 — Structure ECP’AI: business model, governance & partnerships

Lay the strategic foundations of ECP’AI: inclusive governance, a sustainable business model, partnerships… This workshop defines a long-term vision aligned with ECP’s ethical and educational values.

📣 Workshop 2 — Amplify ECP’AI: communication, outreach & advocacy

Explore communication and mobilization levers to inspire, unite, and encourage action for ECP. The workshop strengthens visibility and builds an engaged community around peace and cooperation.

🤖 Workshop 3 — Build the ECP’AI tool: AI for peace

Design ethical, transparent, and eco-aware AI aligned with ECP pedagogy. The goal is to create technology that supports learning, mutual understanding, and the transmission of shared values.

🎓 Workshop 4 — Experiment with ECP’AI: pedagogy & real-data testing

Test, evaluate, and refine AI models and outputs in real educational contexts to ensure alignment with ECP principles. This workshop ensures pedagogical coherence and real-world impact.
