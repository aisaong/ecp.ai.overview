🌍🕊️ ECP’AI Project: Call for Contributions

Let’s build together an innovative pedagogy and an artificial intelligence in the service of peace, education, and humanity 🤝💡

Welcome to ECP’AI’s call for contributions page 💫

This project was born from a simple conviction: AI can be a force for good when it is rooted in the values of Education for a Culture of Peace. Together, let’s build an ecosystem that combines technological innovation with educational wisdom to imagine tools, practices, and learning models with human, ethical, and lasting impact This project stems from a simple conviction: artificial intelligence can be a force for good when rooted in the values of Education for a Culture of Peace 🌿.

🧭 Project Vision

Our ambition is to build a collective, open project that brings together actors in education, technology, and civil society around a common goal: to deploy ECP pedagogy through AI while preserving the fundamental values of living together, benevolence, and solidarity. 🌱.

💡 The 4 Collaborative Workshops

🏗️ Workshop 1 — Structure ECP’AI: business model, governance & partnerships

Lay the strategic. This workshop defines the structural and sustainable vision of the project, ensuring that strategic choices are aligned with ECP’s ethical and educational values.

📣 Workshop 2 — Amplify ECP’AI: communication, outreach & advocacy

Explore the levers of communication, mobilization, and advocacy to inspire, unite, and encourage action in the service of ECP. This workshop aims to strengthen the project’s visibility and to build a committed community around its principles of peace and cooperation.

🤖 Workshop 3 — model, and partnerships

Design ethical, transparent, and eco-aware AI aligned with ECP pedagogy. The goal is to create technology that supports learning, mutual understanding, and the transmission of shared values.

🎓 Workshop 4 — Experiment with ECP’AI: pedagogy & real-data testing

Test, evaluate, and refine AI models and results in concrete educational contexts to ensure their compliance with ECP principles. This workshop ensures the pedagogical coherence of the project and its real impact in educational settings.
